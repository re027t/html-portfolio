The Best Movies According to Mia

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Love in Transit

Two strangers with clashing personalities find themselves stranded together during a blizzard, forcing them to confront their differences and discover unexpected romance.

The Accidental Matchmaker

A well-meaning but clumsy woman inadvertently becomes a matchmaker, wreaking havoc on her friends' love lives while navigating her own romantic entanglements.

Serendipity Café

In a small coastal town, a charming café owner and a free-spirited artist forge a connection through their shared love for art and culinary delights, despite their contrasting life paths.
